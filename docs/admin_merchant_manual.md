# LiveCalicut Operational Manual — Admin & Merchant Guide

## 1. Admin Control Center (`/admin`)

- **Approval Queue**: Review pending business listings, marketplace items, and job postings. Click **Approve** to publish or **Reject** with reasons.
- **Broadcast System**: Publish emergency announcements or festival alerts via `/api/announcements`.
- **System Metrics**: Audit daily active users, subscription MRR, and search trends in `/admin`.

---

## 2. Merchant Operating Workspace (`/merchant`)

- **Profile & Gallery Management**: Configure operating hours, phone contact desk, and upload high-resolution outlet photos in `/merchant/profile`.
- **Offer Deals & Vouchers**: Create discount vouchers and promotional campaign codes in `/merchant/offers`.
- **Customer Inquiry Leads**: Track quote requests and table reservation calls in `/merchant/leads` with 1-click customer call buttons.
- **Subscription Billing**: Manage active Razorpay membership plans (`PRO MERCHANT SUITE`) and download tax invoices in `/merchant/subscription`.
